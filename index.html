About

Meet Dr. Maisa Kamkar

Dr. Maisa Kamkar is the founder of Research Solutions Training and Consultation Center — a dedicated hub for academic and professional researchers across the GCC. With a deep commitment to advancing research culture, Dr. Maisa brings hands-on expertise in research design, scientific writing, and publication strategy.

Her approach combines rigorous academic methodology with practical, AI-powered tools, empowering researchers at every career stage to produce impactful, publishable work.
